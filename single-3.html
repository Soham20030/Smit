GTU Result Analyzer Using Python

GTU Result Analyzer for Faculty" is a specialized platform designed exclusively for faculty members of Gujarat Technological University (GTU).
Utilizing Flask as its backbone, this tool empowers faculty to efficiently access and analyze examination results with ease. With the power of Pandas
for data analysis, faculty members can effortlessly navigate through student performance data, enabling them to make informed decisions to enhance teaching strategies
and curriculum planning. The GTU Result Analyzer for Faculty streamlines the process of result analysis, providing faculty members with a convenient and effective
solution to manage and assess student performance efficiently.
